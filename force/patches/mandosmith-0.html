Mandalorian Smithing Crafting System

Creating a Fire

Chop down trees to obtain wood. Feed it into a stove at your base. Adding more wood increases the temperature, shown on a visual display.

Melting Points

Each type of ore requires a specific temperature to be melted (CURRENTLY ALL THE SAME)

Smithing with a Hammer

Follow these steps to craft your bars:

  1. Pick up raw ore using tongs (Hold ore and click tongs).
  2. Heat up the raw ore by holding it over the fire.
  3. Go over to a table with a hammer (Hold E to hammer).

  5. Cool off the raw ore with a cauldron to return a bar.
